In the fast growing world of technology, we're quickly learning that
what we consume dramatically influences who we are and what we aspire
to be. The people that create the media for the world to consume have
the power to move entire nations. They can project their voice, their
thoughts, and their beliefs across the globe and people will listen.
That's what I want to become - a person whose creations will inspire
others. I believe that GIMM 300 will equip me with some of the
essential tools I'll need to do that.

Projects

Butterfly Blues

For the past three years, I've been a part of the Butterfly Blues comic team, the first published series created by TriSky studio. Haley Wall, Doran McEowen, and myself started the project back in 2016, running exclusively off of Tumblr. Since then, we've expanded our content's availability to additional comic websites as well as Patreon. While my position in the studio is almost exclusively a line-artist, Haley, Doran, and two new collaborators, Casey and Modi, create a variety of fun and engaging additional content such as live streams, gag strips, concept art, and reader requested artwork on our various social media outlets.
